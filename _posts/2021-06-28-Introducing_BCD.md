Hi I am Soumya aka the Blue Coat Dev. This website is a documentation of my musings on technology, games as a medium, anti caste culture and everything in between.

### What Blue Coat Dev is: 

Blue Coat Dev is an attempt to find a community of people who dream of a better society and consider technology to be the medium of their choice. It is a platform to engage in coversations to steer them towards more meaningful arguments. It i also my personal canvas to express my opinions and share my learnings both in terms of technology and culture. I look forward to this journey and I will love you by my side. So feel free to write about your thoughts [here](https://twitter.com/sam_samrules) and I will make sure to address them the first chance I get. Lastly it's about having fun and enjoying every step of the journey, so I'll be regularly posting events from my own life and interesting findings along the way.

### What it is not:

Blue Coat Dev is not a comprehensive reference and should not be used as such. Although I will try to reference all my sources and explain my arguments, at the end of the day I too am a human. I might fall short on depth or worse might be suseptible to my personal biases. If you ever find a point where that happens, just let me know and I assure you my friend that I shall at a moments notice be ready to unlearn, question and relearn.

### Why does it exist?

Right now the future looks uncertain for a lot of us and hope seems to be scarce. I have been hopeless and optimisitic at different points of time and the differences and similarities have intriqued me beyond imagination. This website is a record of the observations I made when hopeless and the musings I've had when hope sprung within me out of nowhere. It's a reminder for me and for you my dear friend that things will get better, all we gotta do meanwhile is take baby steps and if we feel tired, take the rest necessary.

### How to support the website:

Please amplify if you resonate or learn something useful. Be a harsh critic and agood friend. Lastly, have fun using the website.

With Love!
