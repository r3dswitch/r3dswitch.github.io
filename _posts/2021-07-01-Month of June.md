## New hobbies, old traps and borrowed time

Am I am pragmatic not taken away by the chains of emotions or am I just apathetic and ignorant? It's a question that keeps bothering me, perhaps because I know the answer to the question very well. But coming to terms with reality takes a lot of walking. I took the first step of visiting theraphy and it went okayish. I wasn't comprehensive but all I realised was that it would take some time and effort to break out of old traps.

So I took up some new hobbies. Picked up painting and had a great time just playing with paint without any goals in sight. Perhaps that's a metaphor the world used to make me realise the joy of creation is superior than any material possesion. Started creating my body, cultivating my mind and developing a work ethic by starting carpentry.

Getting the offer letter from FMS was the turning point that gave me the fear of falling behind if I don't get back on 'track' immediately. I see sharks now, and I'm loving every moment of it. And I'm going to swim farther than anyone else who has ever given in to sharks ever will.
